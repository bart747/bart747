Mobile menus with many links actually hide content. You can't assume that a site visitor will give you full attention and enough time to read everything carefully. That's why you need to prioritize the key parts of content.

In this example I prioritize product section (In practice, it always depends on context). Visitors who are aleready users familiar with the site might want to log in quickly or find help about the product they use. For them, I used icons and a bit of whitespace to differentiate the links they are looking for.
