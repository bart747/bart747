Forms: Before & After

Typical Signup Form:

Use at least 8 characters including a number

Alerady a user? Sign In.

It's not bad, but if you already have an account, you need to go to other page by clicking a link.

The form is the main thing here, so the “redirect” link is not visible at a glance. A user might be confused also because sign-up and sign-in forms can look similar.

After Redesign:

Create an Account

Use at least 8 characters including a number

Already have one? Log In

Log In

Need to create a new account? Sign Up

Now you can switch forms easily. The appropriate one will appear without the page reload.

Input labels won't confuse you — there's a significant difference between “your password” and “create a new password”. Inputs are also more readable because of more whitespace.
